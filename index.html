Where Theory Meets Application

Since 1995, CEPT has operated as a Center of Excellence at the Faculty of Engineering, Chulalongkorn University, bridging the gap between academic theory and industrial reality. We are dedicated to producing high-quality engineering personnel and serving as a hub for research, development, and technology transfer. We turn complex innovative "know-how" into practical solutions for the society and the power sector.
