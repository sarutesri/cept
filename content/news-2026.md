This week, CEPT had the honor of hosting a delegation of international researchers to discuss future collaborations in **renewable energy integration**.

## Highlights
- Lab tours of our High Voltage testing facility.
- Workshop on "Future of Distributed Energy Resources (DERs)".
- Networking dinner with faculty members.

We look forward to the joint research papers that will emerge from this productive visit.

@title: Collaboration with PEA on Smart Grid
@date: 20 Jan 2026
@category: Announcement
@img: assets/images/news/news-201.jpg
@images: assets/images/news/news-201.jpg, assets/images/news/news-202.jpg

We are proud to announce a new strategic partnership with the **Provincial Electricity Authority (PEA)** to advance smart grid technologies across the region.

## Key Objectives
- **Digital Substation Pilot**: Implementation of IEC 61850 based digital substations.
- **Microgrid Control**: Deploying our custom logic controllers in remote areas.
- **Training**: Upskilling PEA engineers in modern power system analysis.

> "This collaboration marks a significant milestone in bridging academic research with real-world application." - *Assoc. Prof. Dr. Wijarn Wangdee*
